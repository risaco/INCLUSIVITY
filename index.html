  • Race
  • Ethnicity
  • Age
  • Gender
  • Sexual Orientation
  • Abilities
  • Religious Beliefs
  • Political Beliefs

What is tokenism?

What does tokenism look like?

Why is tokenism (or lack of) important?

What can you do to thwart tokenism?

Summary

Tokenism is the practice of limited and exploitative inclusion of members of a minority, underrepresented, or disadvantaged group

You cannot reasonably expect to retain talent simply by making sure they're in all of the pictures.

Mix it up!

What is inclusion?

What does an inclusive team/organization look like?

Why is inclusion important?

What can you do to promote inclusion?

Summary

Inclusion means people, regardless of their abilities, ethnicity, status, or needs are engaged, respected, and appreciated as valuable members of their organization.

The key to creating an inlcusive workplace is engagement!

Mix it up!

What is equity?

What does equity look like?

Why is equity important?

What can you do to promote equity?

Summary

Equity means making sure people are given equal opportunities, equal pay, and fair treatment regardless of their gender, race, disability, religion, nationality, sexual orientation or age.

We must make efforts to level the field so everyone has access.
No favorites.
